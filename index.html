About Me

I am a PhD student at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Bombay. I will be submitting my doctoral thesis soon. My research focuses on efficient simulations of thermoelastic contact problems using advanced computational techniques.

Research Interests

  • Computational Mechanics
  • Reduced Order Modelling
  • Contact Mechanics
  • Fracture Mechanics
  • Nonlinear Mechanics

Thesis Title
